## FAQ

**Q: Is this actually free?**
A: Yes. No credit card, no signup, no catch. We make money when we
connect you with Credex for significant savings — that only happens
if the audit finds real overspend.

**Q: How accurate is the pricing data?**
A: Every price is pulled from official vendor pages and verified
during submission week. We cite sources in our pricing data file.
Prices change — we update weekly.

**Q: Do you store my data?**
A: We store anonymized audit data (which tools, which plans, savings
found). We never store your email unless you explicitly submit it
after seeing your results.

**Q: What is Credex?**
A: Credex sells discounted AI infrastructure credits — Cursor, Claude,
ChatGPT Enterprise and others — sourced from companies that
overforecast their usage. If your audit shows >$500/mo in savings,
Credex can often beat even the optimized pricing.

**Q: What if my stack is already optimized?**
A: We'll tell you honestly. "You're spending well" is a valid audit
result. We'll offer to notify you when better options appear for
your specific stack.
