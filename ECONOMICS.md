
## What is a converted lead worth to Credex?

Credex sells discounted AI credits. Assume:
- Average deal size: $500 (one month of discounted credits for a 5-10 person team)
- Gross margin on credits: ~30% (buying discounted, selling at discount to retail)
- Gross profit per deal: $150
- Repeat purchase rate: 40% monthly (credits are consumable)
- 12-month LTV: $150 + (0.4 × 11 months × $150) = $150 + $660 = **$810 LTV per customer**

Conservative estimate: **$200-400 revenue per converted customer** in first 3 months.

## Conversion funnel math

Assumptions based on similar B2B lead gen tools:
- 1000 visitors/month land on AuditSphere
- 40% complete the audit = 400 audits
- 15% of audit completers enter email = 60 leads
- 20% of leads book a Credex consultation = 12 consultations
- 50% of consultations convert to purchase = 6 customers/month

At $200 revenue per customer (conservative):
**6 customers × $200 = $1,200 MRR from 1,000 monthly visitors**

Break-even: **1 customer/month** covers any future infrastructure costs.

## Path to $1M ARR in 18 months

$1M ARR = ~$83,000 MRR = ~415 customers at $200 average

Month 1-3: 0→50 audits/day via HN, Twitter, IndieHackers
Month 4-6: 50→200 audits/day via widget embeds + word of mouth
Month 7-12: 200→500 audits/day via SEO (pages for each tool comparison)
Month 13-18: 500→1500 audits/day via partnerships with AI tool newsletters

At 500 audits/day × 30 days = 15,000 audits/month
15,000 × 15% email capture = 2,250 leads
2,250 × 20% consultation = 450 consultations
450 × 50% close = 225 new customers/month

225 customers × $200 = $45,000 MRR by month 18 from new customers alone.
With churn at 10%/month and compounding, cumulative ARR crosses $1M around month 16-17.

**What must be true:**
1. Audit quality is high enough that users trust the recommendations
2. Credex consultation booking flow is frictionless (Calendly embed)
3. Widget distribution reaches 20+ AI-focused blogs by month 6
4. Retention: customers come back when their stack changes
